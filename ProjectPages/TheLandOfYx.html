The Land of Yx is a game about switching perspective to manuever around the world!




Switch between moving on the x axis and the z axis of this world, as well as between day and night!




Made with the help of Kelli Norris (BladeOfCadance), Michael Thorson, and Anthony Moncivais for the Global Game Jam 2022
