The Land of Yx

The Land of Yx is a 3D puzzle adventure about switching between two perspectives. This was made for the Global Game Jam with the help of Kelli Norris, Michael Thorson, and Anthony Moncivais. Code was written by Anthony and myself, 3D art was done by Michael, and 2D art was done by Kelli Norris.
