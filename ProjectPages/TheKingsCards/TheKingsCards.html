The King's Cards

The King's Cards is a deck building rougelike created by myself and Garrett Powell. While originally made for mobile devices, we shifted to desktop platforms and published the full game to Steam. All programming was written by Garrett, and all art assets were created by me

Explore 3 unique and randomly generated areas, the dark cave, the fierce mountains, and the mysterious forest
